Goal pages now show **how** a hard goal was solved. When the swarm decomposes a goal into helper sub-lemmas (unsorry ADR-009), both the single-goal view (`/math/goals/<id>`) and the benchmark goal view (`/math/suites/<id>/<goal>`) gain a **"Decomposition — helper lemmas"** section listing each helper (id, status, solver attribution, and a link to its own page) alongside the parent's **composed proof**. The parent→subs relationship is read from unsorry's authoritative `decompositions/*.aisp` records (ingested read-only into the git snapshot), not the fragile `-sN` id suffix, so curated- and suite-pinned subs are covered too. Non-decomposed goals show no section (ADR-037).
