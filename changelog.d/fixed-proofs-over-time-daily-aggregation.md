The **Proofs over time** chart's *merge* basis now aggregates to **one bar per calendar day** instead of plotting the upstream hourly buckets directly (ADR-029 / SPEC-029-A). Because `leaderboard-ui.json.timelines.merge` is bucketed hourly but the chart labelled every point with just its date, a day surfaced as up to 24 same-labelled bars and the latest (current, still-filling hour) bar read as the whole day — so 2026-06-22 looked like ~5 proofs when ~62 had merged. Each day's bar is now the day's summed proofs with the end-of-day cumulative, so the recent slope reflects the real merge cadence. The already-daily *solve* basis is unaffected.
